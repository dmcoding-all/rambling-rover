Ceilidh's are traditional Scottish dances. This was one of the events
thrown for freshmen, and it was the most fun I have had since I arrived.
Most of the dancing is spinning around with your elbows hooked together
with another persons. My roommate and I had no idea what we were getting
ourselves into when we went. While I have none of my own photos (because
I was dancing the entire time), I stole a couple off google that I think
show how much fun it was (and yes! there were a couple of guys wearing
kilts! I'm officially sold). Update: my roommate sent me a video she
took on snapchat of the very last dance and I added it to the photos
above!
