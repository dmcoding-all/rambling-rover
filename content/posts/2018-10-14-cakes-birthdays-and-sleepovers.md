## The Setting

This weekend is Michaela's birthday (on Sunday). So, of course we
went as all-out as we could for her. 

She had two friends (Jeff and Tim) from Wales come in on Friday, and
since we had to house them somehow, I temporarily moved out of my room
and have been having a sleepover with our friend Claire. 

![](https://dcabroad.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/img_7250-1.jpg)Our
matching PJ's were on accident!

## Saturday - Sunrise Hiking hehe

I then convinced everyone on Saturday morning to do a sunrise hike
and consequently wake up at 6:30 am (I don't know why they don't hate me
by now). Turns out it was rainy and cloudy and you couldn't see he
sunrise and yet we still did the hike!

![](https://dcabroad.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/img_7230-e1539520672947.jpg)From left top: Jeff, Tim, Jim, Neil. 
   Bottom left: Me, Claire, Michaela

Then Claire and I went to Flo's place to bake Michalea's favorite
cake: Angel food cake. (Why couldn't it have been something simple like
chocolate cake???). Turns out when you are a poor college student and
you have no kitchen equipment, this leads to you needing to HAND WHIP
THE EGGS. For those of you interested, this took almost an hour and a
million sore arms to do, and some spills happened along the way��

Oh, we also made buttercream frosting for the cake, but because we
were afraid it was too sweet we decided to mix it with whip cream. After
COATING the entire cake with our whip/butter cream creation, we
remembered that Michaela is in fact lactose intolerant... she cannot eat
whip cream. Honestly by this point we had been awake for 6 hours and 4
of those hours had been spent on this cake and we were so done we just
said screw it she can scrape the frosting off the cake. Pretty much
after that the only thing I had time to do was run home, eat real food,
then head to my hockey match. 

### Hockey Match Summary

Unfortunately we lost the match 0-1. My team was a bit frantic and
the result was that we were constantly on our heals the entire first
half and gave up 5 short corners, one of which resulted in the goal.
Second half we were much more composed and even got a couple of our own
shots off but to no avail.

## Birthday Dinner

After the match I ran back and got ready and went and met everyone at
dinner where about 16 friends had all gone out for Michaela's birthday.
I tried a new a meal called a Black Russian (surprisingly very tasty) and
ate Haggis because when in Scotland... (btw I actually love haggis).
Following this, half the group went back early to decorate one of our
common rooms while the other half distracted Michaela for 30 min by
telling her we have to go to the store to buy her lactose free ice
cream. Then we all went back and surprised her with our beautiful (but
to her inedible) cake, and a room filled with signs, homemade bday hats,
and balloons. 

![](https://dcabroad.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/c67be-michaelas-bday-medium.png)

We spent the rest of the night hanging out and enjoying each other's
company before everyone crashed around midnight since we are old souls
and could not rally to make it out to a spot. Therefore: it was a great
night ��
