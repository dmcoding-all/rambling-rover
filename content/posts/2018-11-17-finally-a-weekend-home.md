I feel like I haven't actually had a weekend in Edinburgh for a long
time. So it was a relief when I was actually here this weekend. 

First off, THE SUN BEGAN TO SET AT 3:30! 

CAN WE TALK ABOUT HOW WRONG THAT IS?! 

By 5 PM here it is so dark it feels like its 10 PM, but that being
said, the city is GORGEOUS because everything is lit up. Also, just want
to share my favorite plaque in Edinburgh:

Okay back to the weekend. It started on Friday when Michaela turned
in her essay that she had been stressing about over for the whole week.
We used this as an excuse to get everyone together and celebrate in the
afternoon. Flo has some German friends visiting so they came along too,
and we spent a fun afternoon together![](https://dcabroad.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/img_0047-1-e1542622144229.jpg)

After this Adam left us to go home for the weekend and the rest of us
went home and took a day nap. I literally died. At one point Claire came
in and Michaela and I just passed out and fell asleep before she left.
However, that night we all rallied and went out together to show Flo's
friend the city. We ended up at this pub which was 99% middle aged men
and 1% university students singing Queen songs at the top of their lungs
![](https://dcabroad.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/img_0054.jpg)Then
on Saturday my study abroad program UCEAP had a thanksgiving event for
all students studying at Edinburgh, Glasgow, and London. We had a really
tasty meal, got to go on a tour of the Vaults in Edinburgh, then went to
the play *Shakespeare in Love*.

They also had a photo competition for people to send in 7 photos
sharing what they are thankful for. I sent in a couple photos and
actually won! This was my submission
