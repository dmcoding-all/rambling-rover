Hello to all of you wonderful people, who may be, at this exact
moment, be wondering WHEN IS SHE GOING TO FINISH HER BLOG ABOUT THE AT?
SHE'S GOTTA BE FINISHED BY NOW?!

To all of you wondering the above, I say, you are correct, I HAVE
finished the trail. 

For any of you who may follow me on any of my social media accounts
(Queue shameless plug-ins: [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/theramblingrover/), [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/_the_rambling_rover/), [Youtube](https://www.youtube.com/@RamblingRover), [I-just-created-this-tiktok-so-its-empty-right-now-but-please-follow-me](https://www.tiktok.com/@the.rambling.rover?_t=8hsnvPGlWpe&_r=1)),
you know that my completion of the trail was immediately followed up
with a ultramarathon, and then a cross country road trip. That, combined
with arriving home amidst the holidays has made it hard for me to set
time aside to finish what I am so anxious to share with all of you: the
rest of my story.

Luckily, I was reached out to by Jill Allen (a family friend of my
trail buddy Paddington-whom you may remember). She hosts the podcast: [Find Your Fierce](https://open.spotify.com/episode/2IDIeqpU8g2QCHPlWsu0Ey?si=6f736fa051874e63). She
interviewed me about my time on the trail and the episode was just
released! You can find it [here on Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/find-your-fierce/id1492636273?i=1000637029046) or [here on Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/episode/2IDIeqpU8g2QCHPlWsu0Ey?si=6f736fa051874e63).

I encourage you all to check it out, and know that as you are
listening to it, I am over here on my laptop, finally sitting down and
beginning to write out for you the final installments of my Appalachian
Journey.
