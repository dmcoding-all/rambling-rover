I've read plenty of articles talking about brands with great
warranty's, but I wanted to highlight brands that I have seen/used while
backpacking which I have seen uphold their warranty's. To me, I would
rather buy a product from a brand I know will cover their gear, than one
who will shrug me off when I contact them asking for help.

## Brands with Lifetime Warranty's:

### [Osprey](https://amzn.to/3F7R7i4)

“*Osprey will repair any damage or defect for any reason free of
charge*". I have personally seen Osprey uphold [this warranty](https://www.osprey.com/us/en/customer-support/all-mighty-guarantee) for their
backpacks multiple times on the trail.

### [Outdoor Research](https://amzn.to/3rFchRk)

All Outdoor Research gear is backed by the OR's Infinite Guarantee,
which promises that their “*gear is guaranteed to perform throughout
the lifetime of the product.*”

### [Sea To Summit](https://amzn.to/3Q3QsV1)

"*The [Sea to Summit Guarantee](https://seatosummit.com/pages/warranty) is
our promise to guarantee our products against manufacturing defects in
materials or workmanship for the lifetime of the product.*"

### [NEMO](https://amzn.to/46EfBeh)

"*All NEMO products carry a [lifetime
warranty](https://www.nemoequipment.com/pages/life-time-warranty) against defects in workmanship and materials to the
original owner, with proof of purchase from an authorized NEMO
dealer.*"

### [Outdoor Research](https://amzn.to/3LOYEWC)

[THE INFINITE GUARANTEE](https://www.outdoorresearch.com/pages/warranty)
"*Outdoor Research gear is guaranteed to perform throughout the
lifetime of the product. Our products are built with only the highest
quality standards, rigorously tested, and with your total satisfaction
as our goal. We want to see our customers use our gear to its full
potential, which will include natural weathering from outdoor
environments.*"

### [Patagonia](https://amzn.to/3rGhR61)

Patagonia offers its [Ironclad
Guarantee](https://help.patagonia.com/s/article/Ironclad-Guarantee), promising that “We guarantee everything we make. If you
are not satisfied with one of our products at the time you receive it,
or if one of our products does not perform to your satisfaction, return
it to the store you bought it from or to Patagonia for a repair,
replacement or refund. Damage due to wear and tear will be repaired at a
reasonable charge.”

### [Darn Tough](https://amzn.to/46kyK5d)

Darn Tough's socks are [Unconditionally
Guaranteed for Life](https://darntough.com/pages/our-unconditional-lifetime-guarantee?tw_source=google&tw_adid=650971666707&tw_campaign=12001499950&gclid=CjwKCAjw4P6oBhBsEiwAKYVkq4ushLaRUXqQSaIzVJTyFxp5cCDp98c2UiidGDNvxYThHJuWzXn5jhoCKf8QAvD_BwE&gclsrc=aw.ds) claiming that "*If these aren’t the longest
lasting socks you’ve ever owned, you can return them to us for another
pair. No receipt needed, just the pair in question.*" We hikers go
through socks like its nobody's business. What's great is that you can
just exchange your Darn Tough socks at most outfitters on the trail.

### [North Face](https://amzn.to/3ZMpqVj)

North Face covers its products with a [Limited Lifetime Warranty](https://www.thenorthface.com/en-us/help/returns-policy)
for the lifetime of the product, to the original owner.

## Time-Based or Limited Warranty

### [L.L. Bean](https://www.llbean.com/)

Their [guarantee ](https://www.llbean.com/llb/shop/513705?page=our-guarantee)is "*We stand
behind all our products and are confident that they will perform as
designed. If you are not 100% satisfied with one of our products
purchased directly from L.L.Bean, you may return it within one year of
purchase for a refund. After one year, we will consider any items for
return that are defective due to materials or craftsmanship*."

### [Black Diamond](https://www.amazon.com/stores/BlackDiamondEquipment/page/E6995DD2-94B3-4C1B-A216-B35C1D0F374D?ref_=ast_bln)

"*We trust what we make because we use what we make. Black Diamond
Equipment, Ltd. (“Black Diamond”) warrants to the original end-user
purchaser that its Black Diamond® products will be free from material or
manufacturer defects for the Warranty Periods outlined *."

### [Arc'teryx](https://amzn.to/48K7WwK)

“*Arc’teryx is proud of the quality and performance of all our
products. Items covered under our Limited Warranty will be replaced or
repaired at the discretion of Arc’teryx for the practical product
lifespan. Product damaged through wear and tear, misuse, or neglect may
be repaired at a reasonable price.*”

### [MSR](https://amzn.to/3RS5YEH)

MSR offers a [3-year Limited Warranty.](https://support.cascadedesigns.com/s/article/What-does-MSR-s-Limited-Warranty-cover)

### [Leki](https://amzn.to/3LS8Ihs)

Leki Trekking poles have [the following coverage](https://lekiusa.com/pages/warranty#:~:text=Question%3A%20What%20does%20LEKI%20USA's%20Ski%20Pole%20warranty%20cover%3F,warranty%20covers%20shaft%20breakage%20only.):
"*a one year warranty on our Carbon Shaft Trekking poles and a
lifetime warranty on Aluminum Shaft Trekking poles. **The warranty
covers shaft breakage only.***"

## Shoes

### [Altra](https://amzn.to/3POtRKy)

"Altra provides a [1-year limited warranty](https://www.altrarunning.com/warranty.html) on any manufacturing
defects from the date of purchase on shoes purchased from
altrarunning.com or an authorized Altra retailer. Altra warranty program
does not cover defects due to improper fit, normal wear and tear, damage
due to misuse, alteration to the product, or negligence. Footwear
covered by the Altra warranty will be replaced at Altra’s
discretion.  Replacement is the sole remedy available. 
INCIDENTAL AND CONSEQUENTIAL DAMAGES ARE EXCLUDED.  You may have
other rights that vary by state."

### [Saloman](https://amzn.to/3F7tUfS)

[They’ll give you two years](https://www.salomon.com/en-us/shop/contact/warranty),
and any kind of hard use, for the footwear to die, before which it
is replaced for free.

### [HOKA](https://www.hoka.com/en/us/)

[HOKA warrants](https://www.hoka.com/help-center.html?a=Warranty-Information---id--Y9Li5i7gQqq_0bvuleAKPw) its footwear
products will be free from material defects, damage caused by improper
care prior to the time of purchase, and workmanship defects for one year
from the date of purchase by the original consumer purchaser.

## Thoughts

Many brands well known to the ultralight community, such as Topo,
Gossamer Gear, ULA, etc... I have not included because, while they do
have warranty's, I didn't view them as "great" when compared to these
above. I hope this article helps you when you are next looking to
purchase a product!
