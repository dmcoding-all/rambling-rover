### With Sugar

Contrary to what you may think, you actually WANT SUGAR with your
electrolytes. According to my doctor (who was the first person to tell
me this), and a variety of websites like [this one](https://clinicalherbalism.com/sugar-in-electrolyte-drinks-good-or-bad/), or [this one](https://www.drinkhydrant.com/blogs/news/sugar-as-an-electrolyte), the sugar helps
your body absorb the electrolytes. So you are actually not helping
yourself by taking sugar free electrolytes. You are exercising, you can
afford the calories of the added sugar. That is one of the reasons why
[Liquid IV](https://amzn.to/3F6ZqdK) is such a good electrolyte powder. Another good
option being [DripDrop](https://amzn.to/3LNQZYs), which you can get with or without
sugar.

### With Caffeine

[Nuun + caffeine](https://amzn.to/3RP0URw). Personally I
was not a huge fan of [Nuun electrolyte tablets](https://amzn.to/3LPziIj) because even as I
worked my way through them, I still had a semi-large hard plastic
container I had to deal with. Not to mention there was some time I had
to wait while the tablet dissolved into the water (yes, I know its
moments, but sometimes on the trail I get cranky and my patience gets
thin). However, I found a cannister of the Nuun + Caffeine electrolyte
tablets while in the White Mountains and DANG did they give me the
necessary perk I needed.

### Without Sugar

If you decide you do not want the sugar, your body will still absorb
electrolytes, just maybe not as efficiently, but that is alright,. If
that's what you want, then I can highly recommend the [Gatorade Zero Packets](https://amzn.to/3rJhoQz), they are the lightest electrolyte
packet I brought with me on trail and outside of their lack of sugar,
they have all the good electrolytes for your body.

Another great option, that I think has been highly underrated, are
the [Spartan Hydrate Tablets](https://amzn.to/3tso5a0). Unlike everything else on this
list, these come as tablets that you swallow like pills. I liked this
because of 2 reasons:

- I wear a mouth guard at night and I shouldn't be spending time sugary
colored a meal when I have it in.

- If I a meal a lot of water at night, then I am guaranteed to have to
wake up in the middle of the night, crawl out of my toasty warm sleeping
bag, and go out into the cold night air to pee. No one wants to do that.

Taking the Spartan tablets like pills solves the two above
problems.
