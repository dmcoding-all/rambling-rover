I flew a red eye from LAX to London and then
to Edinburgh, arriving at 9pm. I was blessed to know someone in
Edinburgh, Alex Joyce. My original plan was to somehow get to a hostel
in the Town Centre (probably trying to use a taxi or an uber? I really
had no idea). Luckily, Alex and her boyfriend came and picked me up at
the airport and took me back to her apartment instead. This was probably
for the best since I have a million bags of clothing and one would
likely have gotten stolen if I had stayed in the Hostel
overnight.
