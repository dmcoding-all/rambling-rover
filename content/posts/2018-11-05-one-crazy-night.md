Okay so tonight was perfectly normal until the
end of the night and it just ended so crazily that I have to update you
guys on what happened.
So my roommate (Michaela) has her parents visiting so they took me and
our friend Claire out to dinner at a local pub. Anyways, afterwards us
three were walking back to our dorm (like a 30 min walk) and my stomach
started cramping horribly. I thought I could handle it but it kept
getting worse and worse (I kid you not basically the worst stomach
cramps I have ever experienced). So I just start speed walking because I
KNOW I need the bathroom NOW or things would end POOrly (hehe sorry I
couldn't help but put that in there). But I was also doubling over in
pain every couple minutes and walking bent over which really didn't help
matters.
Okay, so I’m ahead of Michaela and Claire when all of a sudden Claire
calls out my name and I turn around and Michaela is ALSO on the ground.
Turns out, SHE twisted her ankle while walking on the uneven
cobblestones (mind you I twisted my ankle last week)
So then I basically crawl back to them and we are all on the ground
together in the middle of the sidewalk, all in our own versions of pain
and people on the sidewalk are having to walk around these three
hysterical people: I’m laughing/yelling in pain as I am doubled
over from pain in my stomach, Michaela is on the ground because of her
ankle, and Claire is on the ground laughing so hard at the whole
scenario and how crazy it is
So normally I would give Michaela a piggy back but I’m in too much pain
so then Claire has to try and give her a piggy back except that Claire
is also sort of a small human being so it doesn’t work so well![](https://dcabroad.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/img_9309.jpg)
So then us three are slowly making our way back to the dorms, taking
breaks every block and I'm messaging the group chat literally begging
for anyone to come and help

And out of the crowd ADAM comes RUNNING
It was such a moment
And he carries Michaela all the way back to the dorm![](https://dcabroad.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/img_9310.png)
And Claire helps me to limp back to the dorms
Anyways, Adam has ALSO been super sick, so turns out the effort of
sprinting to us made him have a giant coughing fit so bad that he ended
up throwing up. Talk about sacrificing for us.
We then wrapped Michalea's ankle (praise that I had wrap from my twisted
ankle)![](https://dcabroad.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/img_9308.jpg)
