At the beginning of the [Scavenger Hunt ](https://dcabroad.wordpress.com/2018/10/31/scavenger-hunts-and-team-lemons/)completed two weekends
ago, I made a bet with Claire that if my team lost I would dye my
hair.

Well, we lost.

And according to the rules of the bet, I had to put a poll up on my
group chat and they got to vote what color I dyed my hair. What color
did they choose you may ask? [Puce.](https://www.google.co.uk/imgres?imgurl=https://www.macmillandictionary.com/external/slideshow/full/Puce_full.png&imgrefurl=https://www.macmillandictionary.com/dictionary/british/puce&h=1200&w=1800&tbnid=FTa5RjuZnnkIkM:&q=puce&tbnh=160&tbnw=240&usg=AI4_-kSGQs96tVTOFLcFBPEE0BIm0uz1Jw&vet=12ahUKEwjfu8XRzcTeAhUpgHMKHcAXDUMQ_B0wE3oECAUQEQ..i&docid=oT22yH5gEtt6TM&itg=1&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwjfu8XRzcTeAhUpgHMKHcAXDUMQ_B0wE3oECAUQEQ) If you do not know what
puce is, click on the link--or imagine a mix between pink, purple, and
BROWN.

Well last night we dyed my hair. Luckily, puce is not a color often
sold for dying hair (I wonder why), and so my hair turned out more pink
than throw-up colored. And in solidarity, some friends decided to join,
and now everyone who is going to Barcelona this weekend has some form of
dyed hair.
