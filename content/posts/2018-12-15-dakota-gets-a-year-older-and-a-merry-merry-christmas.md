Since my birthday is near the end of December, I asked my friends if
they would celebrate it early with me this year so I could spend my time
with them. They agreed, but since we do not have much time left in the
semester, we squished my birthday celebration together with our
Christmas/Secret Santa.

(BTW: Fairy Tale of New York song is apparently the ultimate Scottish
Christmas song)

We had birthday dinner at Weatherspoons... honestly I do not know how
I have not had Weatherspoons before that day, to quote Neil,
"Weatherspoons is a British past time". Its cheap. The food is good. and
pitchers are only 8 pounds.

I then received the most amazing birthday gift I could have asked for
from these people. They compiled a photo album of our time this past
semester, and everyone wrote in it notes to me about our time together
and random comments on different photos.![](https://dcabroad.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/12/img_0987.jpg)

It was beautiful.

We then had our Secret Santa. It was randomly assigned a couple weeks
back by a computer, but as it turned out, it was extremely rigged. For
example: I got Michaela, my roommate; Kate got Flo, her boyfriend,
George got Claire W, Claire W got Jack, Flo got me, Claire D got
Eleanor, Eleanor got Adam. It was a great mix![](https://dcabroad.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/12/img_1010-e1544905009851.jpg)

And the gifts were amazing. I think each gift was special to the
person who got it. Each time someone opened their gift everyone was just
beaming with smiles at seeing it.

Since Michaela is our resident Historian who gives us history
lessons, (for those wondering its based off a YouTube series and it
turns into Flo and Michaela just telling us fun random facts about the
event). So I personalized a shirt for her which included a lemon in the
lower corner. Flo gave me the ultimate guide to our past semester,
including but not limited to kings cup (ring of fire flat 4 style), the
Appleton tower challenge, and different recipes.

Michaela and Flo then gave us the history of Santa Clause

And the Michaela Flo and Adam performed the dance scene from Mean
Girls (this was priceless to watch)

![](https://theramblingrover.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/12/img_0991-768x1024.jpg)

Overall, I couldn’t have asked for a better night to spend with
friends.
