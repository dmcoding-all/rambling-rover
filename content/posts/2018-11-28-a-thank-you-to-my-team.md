For those who have followed along with my time
here, you know I joined a Field Hockey team , and honestly it was one of
the best decisions I made. It got me to go out and meet new people, and
so many people in the field hockey club have brought me in and made me
feel welcome
There are two groups I want to thank: the 5's and all the the goalies
![](https://dcabroad.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/62f0f77a-08db-4e02-91d8-e9b7117262fc.jpg)
After we beat the 6's. Accidentally wearing my Cal shirt
Honestly, while I am sure I would say this about any team I would be on,
I feel so lucky to have ended up on the 5's. They are the type of people
that bring you in and make you feel welcome. I felt that there was a
good balance between having a fun time, enjoying playing the sport we
love, and also putting pressure on ourselves to win games and play our
best. Socials were a blast. The team always looked out for each other,
and one of my favorite parts of each week was Thursday morning when the
entire team would be messaging trying to piece together what happened
the night before. (legit so entertaining)
