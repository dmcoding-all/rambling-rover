Claire, Michaela, and I went to Dublin the weekend of December
14-16

Yes I still had two finals. No I had not studied for either one of
them. No I probably shouldn't have gone on this trip. But yes I was glad
I was going.

That is until we had to wake up at 5am and after staying up till 1am
the night before for the ceilidh. due to this, we all ended up brushing
our teeth in the airport like real professional study abroad weekend
travelers ![](https://dcabroad.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/12/img_1237.jpg)

Despite running on only >4 hrs of sleep, we managed to get
ourselves to the airport on time. This was our first time traveling with
Michaela which was great cause Claire and I had done most of the stuff
before and we were able to sort of show her the ropes. Her reaction upon
discovering that Ryan Air has you physically walk outside to the plane
was GREAT
