Edinburgh was my home for three months during my semester abroad at the University of Edinburgh — and it remains one of my favourite cities in the world. It's walkable, dramatically beautiful, and packed with history around every corner. The Old Town and New Town feel like two completely different cities stacked on top of each other. I arrived in September knowing almost no one and left in December with some of the best friends I've made travelling. If you get the chance to spend real time here — not just a weekend — take it.
